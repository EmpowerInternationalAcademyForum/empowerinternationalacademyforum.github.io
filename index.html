Explore Our Clubs & Activities

At EIA, we offer a diverse range of clubs and extracurricular activities designed to nurture students' talents and interests. From arts and drama to technology and sports, there's something for everyone.

IT Club - A haven for creative minds to express themselves through painting, sculpting, and design.

Debate Club - Where innovation meets collaboration, building the engineers of tomorrow.

Music Dance & Drama Club - Promoting teamwork and athletic excellence on and off the court.

Chess Club - Sharpen your mind and strategy skills in the timeless game of chess.

Environment Club - Advocating for a greener planet through practical environmental initiatives.

Farmers Club - Cultivating knowledge and skills in sustainable agriculture and farming.

Innovation Club - Inspiring creativity and technological solutions for real-world problems.
